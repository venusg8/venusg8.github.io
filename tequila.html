Tequila Sunrise Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups ice
  • 2 fluid ounces of tequila
  • 1 cup ice
  • 4 fluid ounce orange juice
  • 3/4 fluid ounce grenadine

Preperation

Fill a highball glass with 1 1/2 cups ice and set aside. Combine tequila and orange juice in a cocktail mixing glass. 1 cup ice, stir, and strain into the prepared highball glass. Slowly pour in grenadine and let settle. Stir before drinking.
