Gin Cocktail Recipes

Gin Garden

Ingredients

  • 1 inch English cucumber peeled
  • 2 fl oz Ruttle Dry Gin
  • 1 fl oz St German Elderflower Liqueur
  • 1 fl oz Pressed apple juice

Preparation

Muddle cucumber in base of shaker. Add other ingredients, SHAKE with ice and fine strain into chilled glass.

Gin and Mint

Ingredients

  • 50ml gin
  • Two tender top sprigs of fresh mint, washed
  • 1cm thick slice fresh cucumber, quartered
  • 100m chilled elderflower pressé (NB: not concentrate)
  • Small mint sprig and thin lemon slice to garnish

Preperation

Place the gin, mint and cucumber pieces in the bottom of a cocktail shaker or glass jug, and stir for a minute, squashing down the mint and cucumber with the back of a spoon to bruise them well and release their juices. Strain into a tall thin tumbler containing a couple of ice cubes, and top up with spritzy elderflower pressé. Garnish with mint and lemon.

Moon Mountain Greyhound

Ingredients

  • Salt (optional)
  • 1.25 oz Moon Mountain Wild Raspberry Flavored Vodka
  • .25 oz Elderflower liqueur
  • 2 oz Ruby red grapefruit juice
  • .25 oz Orgeat syrup

Preperation

If desired, coat the rim of a rocks glass with salt. Fill with ice and set aside. Add the remaining ingredients to a shaker and fill with ice. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass. Garnish with a grapefruit peel and/or a fresh rosemary sprig.

Tom Collins

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Old Tom or London Dry gin
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • ¾ ounce lemon juice
  • Soda water
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish
  • Cocktail cherry, for garnish

Preperation

Shake gin, syrup and juice with ice until chilled, about 15 seconds. Strain into an ice-filled highball. Top with soda water. Garnish with lemon wheel or wedge and a cherry.
